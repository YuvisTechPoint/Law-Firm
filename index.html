Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our legal services

How do I find the right advocate for my case?
Use our smart search to filter advocates by practice area, city, and experience. Each profile includes detailed qualifications, case history, and client reviews. You can book a free initial consultation to assess compatibility before committing.
What legal practice areas do you cover?
We cover 40+ practice areas including Family Law, Criminal Defense, Property Disputes, Corporate Law, Cyber Crime, Consumer Protection, GST/Tax, Intellectual Property, NRI Services, PIL, RERA, Labour Law, Arbitration, and much more.
Where are your professional offices located?
Our primary offices are located near the Calcutta High Court, with associate offices in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, and Chennai. Our network of advocates spans across all major Indian cities and High Court jurisdictions.
What are the professional fee structures?
We offer flexible consultation packages starting from ₹0 (free initial consultation) to ₹2,499 for corporate sessions. Case representation fees vary by complexity, court level, and duration. All fees are quoted transparently before engagement.
What distinguishes your legal services?
We combine 20+ years of collective legal expertise with modern client service practices — 24/7 availability, case tracking, transparent billing, and dedicated client managers. Our 4.9★ Google rating reflects our commitment to excellence in every case.
How does the payment process work?
Consultation fees are paid securely via Razorpay — accepting UPI, cards, net banking, and wallets. Case representation fees are structured as a retainer + milestone model with GST-compliant invoices provided for all payments.
Is my consultation confidential?
Absolutely. All communications with our advocates are protected by attorney-client privilege under the Advocates Act, 1961. Your personal information and case details are never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
Can I consult online?
Yes! All consultations can be conducted via video call (Google Meet / Zoom), phone, or WhatsApp. We serve clients across India and NRI clients worldwide. After booking, you receive a confirmation with the meeting link.
